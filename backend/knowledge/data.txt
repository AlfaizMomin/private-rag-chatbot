ALFAIZ TECHNOLOGIES — COMPANY KNOWLEDGE BASE
=============================================

## Company Overview
Alfaiz Technologies is a software development company founded in 2021 and headquartered in Pune, India.
The company specializes in building artificial intelligence, machine learning, and full-stack web development solutions.
Alfaiz Technologies currently employs 48 people across engineering, design, sales, and operations departments.
The company's mission is to deliver intelligent, scalable, and privacy-focused software to businesses of all sizes.

## Products & Services
Alfaiz Technologies offers four core product lines:

1. AlfaizAI Studio — A no-code AI model builder platform that lets businesses train and deploy custom ML models without writing code. It supports classification, regression, and NLP tasks.

2. AlfaizChat — A white-label, on-premise conversational AI platform powered by large language models. It can be self-hosted and requires no external API calls, making it fully private.

3. AlfaizInsight — A real-time business intelligence dashboard that connects to databases, cloud services, and spreadsheets to generate automated reports and charts.

4. AlfaizCloud — A managed private cloud infrastructure offering for Indian SMEs that provides compute, storage, and networking on dedicated hardware.

## Pricing
- AlfaizAI Studio: Starts at ₹4,999 per month for up to 5 models. Enterprise plans are custom-quoted.
- AlfaizChat: Starts at ₹9,999 per month. Includes unlimited messages and up to 3 custom knowledge bases.
- AlfaizInsight: Starts at ₹2,499 per month for up to 10 data connectors.
- AlfaizCloud: Pricing starts at ₹1,499 per month for a 2 vCPU / 4 GB RAM instance.

## Technology Stack
- Backend systems are built primarily with Node.js, Python (FastAPI), and Go.
- Frontend applications use Angular and React.
- AI/ML pipelines use PyTorch, LangChain, and ONNX Runtime for inference.
- Databases used: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Redis.
- Infrastructure runs on a mix of AWS and dedicated bare-metal servers.

## Certifications and Compliance
- Alfaiz Technologies is ISO 27001 certified for information security management.
- All products are GDPR-compliant and follow India's DPDPA (Digital Personal Data Protection Act) guidelines.
- The company completed SOC 2 Type I audit in March 2024 and is pursuing SOC 2 Type II.
